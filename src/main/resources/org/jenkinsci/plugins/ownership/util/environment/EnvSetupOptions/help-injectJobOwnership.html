Inject following variables:
  • JOB_OWNER - UserId of the job owner
  • JOB_OWNER_EMAIL - E-mail of the job owner
  • JOB_COOWNERS - Comma-separated list of job co-owners (includes owner)
  • JOB_COOWNERS_EMAILS - Comma-separated list of e-mails of job co-owners(includes owner's e-mail)
All variables may be empty if there's no ownership info available.
