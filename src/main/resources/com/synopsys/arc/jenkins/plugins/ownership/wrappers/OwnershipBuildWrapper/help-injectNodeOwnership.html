Inject following variables:
  • SLAVE_OWNER - UserId of the slave owner
  • SLAVE_OWNER_EMAIL - E-mail of the slave owner
  • SLAVE_COOWNERS - Comma-separated list of the slave owners(includes owner)
  • SLAVE_COOWNERS_EMAILS - Comma-separated list of e-mails of the slave owners(includes owner's e-mail)
All variables may be empty.
